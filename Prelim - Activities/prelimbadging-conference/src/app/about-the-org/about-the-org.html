Our
Mission

We believe in transparency, cooperation globally, and the free exchange of talent and ideas. A forum for the sharing of these technical and humanitarian discussions is given for meetings, conferences and other events.

We are also committed to creating a secure, constructive and supportive atmosphere for all participants in conferences, activities and meetings who participate in these discussions with us.

Conferences are a part of the legacy of this organization and continue today to be a catalyst for creativity. We are committed to fostering innovation, collaboration, and academic excellence across all disciplines and geographical boundaries.
